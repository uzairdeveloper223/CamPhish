## Purpose

CamPhish is a security research and penetration testing tool developed to simulate camera phishing attack vectors. It exists to help security professionals, researchers, and educators understand how such attacks are conducted so that they may build better defenses against them.

This tool is intended solely for:

- Authorized penetration testing engagements
- Cybersecurity education and academic research
- Controlled lab environments and personal test setups
- Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions
- Security awareness training with full participant consent
